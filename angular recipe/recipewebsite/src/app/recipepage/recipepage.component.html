Instructions:

  1. Bring water to a boil in saucepan on med-high heat.
  2. While water is heating, in a separate bowl mix the gocujang, red pepper flakes, and brown sugar. Cut 2 green onion stalks into 1 inch sections.
  3. Once water is boiling, add the gochujang mixture in the saucepan until fully incorporated.
  4. Add in the defrosted deok and green onions and stir.
  5. Leave saucepan uncovered and lower heat to med-low. Stir occassionally. Let simmer for 25 minutes.
  6. (Optional) Cut fishcake sheets into squares and add into saucepan 10 minutes before it is done.
  7. Transfer to a serving dish and top with minced green onions. Serve while hot!
